```python

    return P * (1 + r/n) ** (n * t)

print(compound_interest(1000, 0.05, 12, 10))
```

# Financial Formula

Compound interest formula:

$$
A = P \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}
$$

Where:

- $P$ = principal
- $r$ = annual interest rate
- $n$ = times compounded per year
- $t$ = years
